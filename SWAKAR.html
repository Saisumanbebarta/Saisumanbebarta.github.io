SWAKAR

Team Members: Tanvi Mallick and Sai Suman Bebarta

Problem Solved:

These days waste disposal is a major concern around the world. Wherever we go, we see tons of refuse lying around. Although to keep our surroundings clean, we have been taught to pick up litter wherever we see it we don’t actually do so. We believe to eradicate this problem, as the future citizens of our country and the world, children need to be made aware about their responsibility to battle irresponsible garbage disposal and littering. But no matter how many textbooks they read, children learn best through activities. However, picking up litter might seem like a boring and mundane task to children. This is the problem we are trying to solve. We aim to do this through combining innovation and toys.
