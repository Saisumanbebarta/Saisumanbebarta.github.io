FARM-O-BOT

Team Members: Tanvi MallicK and Sai Suman Bebarta

Problem Solved:

As we all know food matters for everyone . But in this world not everyone gets food .In other words not everyone has a job .There are many reasons of being jobless .Three such reasons being-illness, gender discrimination and aging. There are many poor handicapped people who cannot earn their livelihood, there are women who cannot feed their babies and then there are old people who are suffering .People, who do not have a job, do not have food either and have to go through starvation almost every other day. So we decided to make a robot to solve this big problem. When we think about the most basic job in India then the answer immediately comes to our mind-AGRICULTURE. Agriculture is such a job that needs a lot of hard work but ultimately will lead to a sweet end. But if we reduce the hard work and make agriculture easy then it will be very helpful. So we made a robot that will make agriculture downright easy.
