SNAP SENSE

Our device offers independence to the blind by describing their surroundings. In a visually dominated world, this innovation provides real-time information, liberating individuals from constant assistance. It's not just about accessibility; it's a tool for active engagement, empowering the blind to navigate and understand their environment autonomously.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the surrounding captured?

A: The device uses a camera which captures the surrounding and send them for processing.

Is the device portable?

A: Yes, the device is designed to be compact and portable, allowing users to carry it easily wherever they go and attach wherever they want.

Can the device be used indoors and outdoors?

A: Yes, the device is versatile and can be used effectively both indoors and outdoors. Yet it needs some software update time to time for better experience.

How accurate is the distance measurement feature?

A: The distance measurement feature is 90% accurate, providing precise information to help users navigate safely that to be with no extra sensors.

What is the future development plan for the device?

A: A compact, powerful, effective and long battery life version of the current version.
