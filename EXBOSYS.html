EXBOSYS

Team Members: Sai Swarup Bebarta, Saswat Subhamanu Pradhan and Sai Suman Bebarta

Problem Solved:

Nowadays, there is water scarcity in the world and many buildings are catching fire and normally, they are using water sprinklers for extinguishing fire where a lot of water is wasted with the wastage of time. But if the fire is caused due to petroleum or shot circuit, we cannot use water sprinklers as a common extinguisher. For that reason now a day's people are using fire extinguishing bomb for extinguishing fire but it is very harmful as it contains compressed carbon dioxide gas and if a person inhales this gas he/she may die. But there is no system made till now which can launch the bomb automatically towards the fire at the minimum time; using the maximum speed to transmit the data to the necessary addresses like the fire station, neighboring area, family members ; to prevent the human beings from injury or suffocation. And if we use the fire extinguishing bomb without our system, then the room will be covered with carbon dioxide gas which will cause a lot of suffocation in the room and even cause death if the person is unable to come out of the room in perfect time or if the person has not worn the safety masks and the glasses.
